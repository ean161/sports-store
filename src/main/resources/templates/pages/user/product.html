PURCHASE GUIDE

To buy immediately, simply call 0963.51.41.31 and provide the product name and desired size.

Step 1: Visit our website and choose the product you want to buy

You can use the size filter to quickly find the model that fits you best.

Step 2: Click on the product you want to buy

Click the BUY NOW red button and don’t forget to select your size.

Step 3: Fill in your order information

Your phone number and detailed address are very important, so please double-check after filling them in.

Step 4: Review and submit your order

After receiving your order, SPORTS-STORE will usually call you immediately, so please keep an eye on your phone.

HOW TO CHOOSE SIZE

Method 1: Based on your current shoes (95% accuracy)

Take out your current pair of shoes and check the size (if you rarely play football, you can borrow from a teammate or neighbor :D)

If you’re wearing fabric shoes (Thuong Dinh, Thashoco, Bata Warrior, etc.), you should go up 1 size when choosing.

(Example: If you wear Thuong Dinh size 39, choose size 40)

If you’re wearing Nike, Adidas, Puma, etc., keep your current size.

Method 2: Based on the size chart (80% accuracy)

If you don’t have a pair of shoes nearby, you can measure your foot and compare it with the chart below.

Shoe Size Chart

Method 3: Call the SPORTS-STORE hotline - 0963.51.41.31 for assistance

If the above two methods still don’t help, don’t hesitate to call SPORTS-STORE for assistance.

If your shoes bought at SPORTS-STORE don’t fit, you can always exchange them until they fit perfectly.

RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY

1. Exchange due to personal preference (unsatisfied with the product)

All purchased items cannot be refunded but can be exchanged within 7 days from the date of receipt.

SPORTS-STORE only accepts exchanges for products that meet the following conditions:

  • Remain in their original condition (surface not wrinkled, studs not scratched or worn)
  • Include all accessories or gifts that came with the order
  • Shoes must be unused (not even for trial running or playing)

2. Exchange not due to customer’s fault

SPORTS-STORE encourages customers to check the package and product condition before payment.

If you find that the goods are not in perfect condition, incorrect, or missing items, please take photos and contact SPORTS-STORE immediately.

3. Notes when sending exchange items

  • Only send the item that needs exchange or warranty, usually no need to send accessories.
  • Customers cover the shipping cost when sending the item

4. Refund methods

  • Exchange for a new product of the same type
  • Bank transfer refund
  • Cash refund at the store
  • Refund via mobile phone top-up card

NOTE: ALL PRODUCTS IN SALE / CLEARANCE / GIFT PROGRAMS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR RETURNS OR EXCHANGES.
